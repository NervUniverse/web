Our Story

Welcome to Nerv Glorious, where our vision is to connect people from all walks of life through the power of gaming. Founded on March 20, 2023, by a passionate gamer with a dream to create a vibrant and inclusive gaming community.

At Nerv Glorious, we believe that esports is more than just a competitive industry – it’s a platform for building meaningful connections and relationships. Our goal is to create a safe and positive space where gamers of all backgrounds can come together and share their passion for gaming.

Vision & Mission

Our vision is to adopt the highest standards of ethics and professionalism in every aspect of our operations, ensuring transparency and honesty in every interaction and decision, prioritizing the safety and comfort of the e-sports community.

Our mission is to help teams achieve success with the best facilities, skill development support, and partnerships for knowledge exchange.
